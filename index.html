Crab Movement

We all know crabs move horizontally. The question is: can crabs move vertically forward or backward? Some friends will tell me the crab can zigzag move forward. That's a very important point and we will show you how to make profits by learning from the crabs.

What's trading?

You trade apple with orange. What's happening? You get more oranges, but your apples decrease. The fundamental character about trading is: When one asset increases, The other asset must decrease. Now let's make some definition:

  • When the quantity of one asset increase while the other decrease, we call it left or right horizontal movement.
  • When both quantities of the assets increase, we call it vertical forward movement.
  • When both quantities of the assets decrease, we call it vertical backward movement.

  • At point O, you have zero apple and zero orange.
  • At point X, you have all oranges.
  • At point Y, you have all apples.
  • At point W, you have both oranges and apples.

What's trading again?

When you trade, your assets move from one point to another point in the diagram. Two points construct a directional line. The direction is roughly X->Y or Y->X direction. We call it horizontal direction.

What we are interested is the other direction O->W direction. We call it verticall forward movement. If your assets move from O to W point, Your assets will increase for sure.

Our trading always do horizontal movement. Can you build a machine to move in O->W direction?
