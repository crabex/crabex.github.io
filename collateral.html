CrabEx Dapp

CrabEx is a collateral oriented, safe, solid and free financial system running on libra blockchain. Why do we make it collateral oriented?

Let's consider a very simple scenario. Alice wants to exchange an apple coin with Bob for an orange coin. Alice deliver the apple to Bob right now. However Bob will only deliever the orange to Alice one month later. Bob promises to Alice that he will do it. Can Alice trust it? If Alice trusts it and sign the contract, it may result in a lot of conflict in the future since Bob may default. If a financial system is built on promise, the system will doom to fail sooner or later. We need a concrete and safe system. What's the robust system built oriented? We will make a very important statement here:

Promise is cheap and Collateral is valuable!

Let's rework our example above. This time we will use our collateral oriented principle.

  1. Alice promises to deliver one apple coin to Bob 6 months later. She locks 1000 USDT as collateral on the blockchain.
  2. Bob promises to deliver one orange coin to Alice 6 months later. He locks 1000 LIBRA as collateral on the blockchain.
  3. On the contract mature date, If Alice defaults, she will lose 1000 USDT. If Bob defaults, he will lose 1000 libra.

Is this contract more solid than the contract without collateral?

We build all our financial contract collateral oriented. Every contract will lock some kind of collaterals. Every contract is financial asset itself. So every contract can be locked as collateral for another financial contract. It is a chain of collaterals. So our financial system is safer and more solid than most of the financial system in the world.

Bootstrap blockchain collaterals

Now we know we have a concrete blockchain financial system which is built on collaterals. We still need to worry about what the initial seed collaterals are. Actually it comes from real assets or credits off-chain. We must have all kinds of authority who acts as the immediate agent. The authority issue you the on-chain tokens when you deposit the off-chain assets as off-chain collaterals to the authority. Just a few examples here:

  • You deposit a basket of fiat currency as collateral to libra organization leading by facebook, then you get libra back.
  • You have credit with a credit card company like VISA or MASTERCARD. Then you get credit token on the blockchain
  • You deposit your house certificate as collateral and sign a contract with a bank. You now get house token assets on the blockchain.

If we think off-chain assets as slow assets running locally in a slow way, then all collateral oriented blockchain assets are running on a solid safe efficient highway. The authority acts highway entry and exit agents.
